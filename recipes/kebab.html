Kebab recipe

Description

This is a easy and quick recipe, to make a basic lasagne. It can take some time because if you want the meat as good as possible, you slow cook it a while. But it's worth it :)

Ingrediense

  • Kebab bread
  • Kebab meat
  • Spices
  • Salad
  • Corn
  • Tomato
  • Dressing

Steps

  1. cook the meat with the spices
  2. Cut the vegtables
  3. Add dressing
  4. Roll the meat and vegtables in the bread
