Hamburger recipe

Description:

This is a recipe for how to make a delicuous burger. This is an quite easy and quick recipe with few ingrediouns and steps. To follow this recipe you don't need alot of equitment. A oven, frying pan, knife and spetucla is enough.

ingrediens

  • Hamburger buns
  • Beef burger
  • Dressing
  • Onion
  • Salad
  • Tomato

steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Cook the beef burgers
  3. Cut the tomato, onion and salad
  4. Warm the burger buns
  5. Between the buns place the burger, and vegtebales
  6. On the top add the dressing
  7. Last step was to add the top bun
